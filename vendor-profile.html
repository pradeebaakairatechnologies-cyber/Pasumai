Pasumai Palangal is a trusted family-owned business in Tuticorin that has been providing high-quality fruits for three generations. Our store, previously known as POP Chellam Vilas Fruit Commission Mandi, is now owned by P. Chella Ganesan, the son of Late POP Pulendra Pandian. We offer a wide variety of fruits, Regular, Exotic, Tropical and Imported fruits, all at affordable prices.

We also provide customized packaging for freshly made juices, shakes, and cut fruits, in addition to selling dry fruits, nuts, honey and dates. You can conveniently order from us online through our own website www.pasumaipalangal.com, Swiggy and Zomato. Choose Pasumai Palangal for delicious, fresh and affordable fruits and juices that are worth your money.
