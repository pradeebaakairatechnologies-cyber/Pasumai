Yes, all products sold at Pasumai Palangal are sourced directly from trusted local farmers who follow natural and chemical-free farming practices. We personally verify each supplier to ensure that no harmful pesticides or artificial fertilizers are used, so you can buy with complete confidence.

Placing an order is simple. Browse our shop, add your desired items to the cart, and proceed to checkout. You can choose from multiple payment options including UPI, net banking, and cash on delivery. Once your order is confirmed, you will receive an SMS and email confirmation.

We currently deliver across Thoothukudi (Tuticorin) and nearby areas in Tamil Nadu. We are actively expanding our delivery network. If you are unsure whether we deliver to your location, please contact us at +91 94444 30999 or visit our Contact Us page.

There is no strict minimum order quantity for regular customers. However, for bulk orders or wholesale purchases, we recommend a minimum order of ₹500 to qualify for special discounts and free home delivery. Please contact us for bulk pricing and availability.

We accept a wide range of payment methods including UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), net banking, debit/credit cards, and cash on delivery. All online transactions are secured with industry-standard encryption to keep your payment information safe.

Your satisfaction is our priority. If you receive damaged, spoiled, or incorrect items, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery with a photo of the product. We will arrange a replacement or issue a full refund promptly. Returns are not required for perishable goods.

We welcome local farmers and organic produce sellers to join our platform. Simply visit our Vendor page and register your interest. Our team will review your application and get in touch with you within 2–3 business days to guide you through the onboarding process.

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive an SMS notification with your delivery details. You can also log in to your account and visit the Order History section in your dashboard to track the status of your order in real time. For further assistance, call us at +91 94444 30999.
