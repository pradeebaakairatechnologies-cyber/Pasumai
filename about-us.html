FRESH FROM OUR FARM

Thoothukudi's Trusted Organic Store

We are committed to bringing the finest fresh fruits, fruitss, and organic produce directly from local farms to your doorstep — with zero chemicals and zero compromise.

Pasumai Palangal has been a trusted name in Thoothukudi for three generations. Founded as a small family-owned fruit stall, we have grown into a full-fledged organic store serving thousands of households. Every product we carry is sourced from verified natural farmers, ensuring you always get the freshest, most nutritious produce for your family's table.

Grown Without Chemicals, Purely Natural

Every product at Pasumai Palangal is sourced from farms that follow 100% natural growing methods — no synthetic pesticides, no artificial fertilizers, and no harmful chemicals. What you get is pure, wholesome produce exactly as nature intended, full of real nutrition and flavour.
