Our Journey

Pasumai Palangal was born in 2018 from a simple idea at our home in Tuticorin to bring fresh, premium, and healthy fruits directly to our community.

Our roots go much deeper than that. We come from a proud five-generation fruit wholesale business family, operating under the legacy Trade Mark and Name POP .M/s. Chellam Vilas Fruit Commission Mandi is one of the pioneers in quality fruit wholesaling. For decades, our family has been deeply connected with fruit growers, markets, and 200 + vendors around Tuticorin District.
